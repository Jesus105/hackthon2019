business model

Mission:

Look For Her is a company that generates, drives and develops technological solutions through high quality mobile application services committed to helping our users achieve greater security in their well-being, always ensuring their personal integrity. Our purpose is to raise awareness in society to support the fight against gender violence.

View:

We have set out to cross borders and have a reach and impact on women worldwide by expanding support services and information technology, in all centers to help women, as a highly competitive and reliable company

Executive Summary:

Look For Her is committed to the fight against gender violence, in the same way with its active users of the platform. Business description: Look For Her has a brand sponsorship in favor of gender equality, as well as a Premium User option that will allow our partners greater flexibility and efficiency within the mobility of the application.

Market research:

Ideally, the application aims to reach women of all ages around the world, but the main audience of our application are women who feel vulnerable or threatened in their social environment.

Competition Analysis

We believe in the value of our ideas, entrepreneurship, and the importance and strength that the technological world represents today, we want to bet on an idea that represents more than a mobile application. We seek to be generators of technological solutions in a traditional market with serious management and administrative management, with clear policies and strategies driven by intelligence and passion. We see this as an opportunity to generate a real project of an idea, the union of our knowledge, our desire to be the best and achieve an impact on society.

Financing of the company:

To make the project a reality it is necessary to have a seed capital of $ 5,000 dollars with which it is intended to start generating profits in just 2 months. The estimated time to obtain the return on investment is 6 months with a return of 60%.

Sales strategy:

For the users of the application it is intended that there are types of accounts, the standard account and the premium account. In the standard account, only limited functions of the application can be accessed, in addition to advertising for which third parties can generate income. On the other hand, the premium account can access all the functions of the application and without advertising, with a subscription of $ 20 per month.
